Flip

Flips the current image either horizontally, vertically, or both.

Requests are passed thus: 

<!--Horizontal-->
http://your-image?flip=horizontal
<!--Vertical-->
http://your-image?flip=vertical
<!--Both-->
http://your-image?flip=both
