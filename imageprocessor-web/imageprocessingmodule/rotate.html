Rotate

Rotates the current image by the given angle without clipping.

Requests are passed thus: 

<!--Standard-->
http://your-image?rotate=26
<!--With background color-->
http://your-image?rotate=54&bgcolor=fff
<!--Legacy with background color-->
http://your-image?rotate=angle-54,bgcolor-fff
